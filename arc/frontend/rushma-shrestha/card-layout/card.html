eSewa App

eSewa is Nepal's first digital wallet and online payment platform, launched in 2009 and operated by F1Soft International. Licensed by Nepal Rastra Bank, it allows users to make a wide range of payments conveniently through their mobile phones or computers. With eSewa, users can recharge mobile phones, pay utility bills (like electricity, water, and internet), book travel tickets, pay for school fees, insurance premiums, and even make payments to government offices. It also supports bank transfers, online shopping, and QR code payments at participating merchants.
